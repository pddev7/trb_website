Namshkar, We, TheRedbuttons are a Music Production Company who is attempting 1st ever India Book of Record in the category of Dance for "Most Number of Participants in a dance performance on a digital platform"

All we need are an enthusiastic and determined dancers to set this amazing national record.

We want everyone to dance on Kathak with us. NO FORMAL EDUCATION REQUIRED IN KATHAK.
Difficulty level :- Beginner Level Kathak.
Length of the performance :- 90 secs
Record can be attempted Individually as well as in form of Group Dance (Maximum 30 in group)
Register Yourself or your Group on theredbuttons.com or find link on our social media.
After registration you will get access to the choreography. You can watch it and learn it yourself. For guidance in learning Choreography please contact us on our website.
All participants have to be present for the major 3 online Rehersals. Those who miss 2 rehersals will be left out of the performance.
No Registration fee.
After we successfully set the IBR record, all participants/ groups will be provided with Pretigous IBR Certificates, certificate from us and merchandise. This all will be shipped on address provided by you.
